How many calories are there in a 320ml can of Coca-Cola Zero Sugar?



Less than 1 calorie.


Coca-Cola Zero Sugar contains a blend of low-calorie sweeteners (sucralose and acesulfame-K), which means it can be sugar- and calorie-free, without compromising on taste.
